Flourless Chocolate Torte

Ingredients

  • 9 oz dark chocolate, (65% cacao or higher, chopped finely
  • 9 oz unsalted butter
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 7 eggs, at room temperature
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp almond extract
  • Optional - powdered sugar
  • Optional - berries (I like strawberries and raspberries with this torte)
  • Optional - whipped cream

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375o F. Grab a 9-inch springform pan and grease it. (A regular baking dish this size works as well. I would recommend a pie dish if you don't have a springform pan.)
  2. Melt the chocolate and butter together in a double boiler OR in a microwave-safe bowl. Heat up the mixture until chocolate is almost completely melted. Remove from heat and mix until completely melted.
  3. Then add the granulated sugar and mix until combined. Set aside to cool for a few minutes.
  4. Add the eggs, one at a time, combining fully after each egg. Once you've added them all, continue to stir the batter until it becomes thick and glossy.
  5. Add the vanilla and almond extract and mix until just combined.
  6. Pour the batter into the greased pan. Bake for about 30-35 minutes until the torte jiggles slightly in the middle but is not completely set. Remove from the oven and let it cool in the pan for at least 10 minutes. If you use a springform pan, you can remove the torte. Otherwise, I would recommend leaving it in the pan it is in.
  7. Cut slices and serve with any toppings you prefer, and enjoy!
