Chicken Tortilla Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 small yellow onion
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 3-5 garlic cloves
  • 2 chicken breats
  • 16 oz can of corn, drained
  • 2 tbsp of taco seasoning
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 16 oz can diced tomatoes
  • 16 oz can black beans, drained
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • Avocado, sliced (optional)
  • Cheese, shredded (optional)
  • Cilantro (optional)
  • Lime wedges (optional)
  • Tortilla chips (optional)

Directions:

For this recipe, I use the Pampered Chef Quick Cooker. It is not necessary, but the directions will be for this device.

  1. Dice the onion and bell pepper. Prepare the garlic and mince it.
  2. Heat oil in the Quick Cooker on the Sear setting.
  3. Once oil is hot, add the diced ingredients and cook uncovered until soft about 3-5 minutes.
  4. Add the garlic.
  5. When the garlic is fragrant, add chicken, corn, seasoning, salt, tomatoes, and black beans.
  6. Stir ingredients and close lid.
  7. On the Quick Cooker, use the Custom setting. Adjust the time to 10 minutes and press Start.
  8. When done, remove chicken and shred it.
  9. Change the Quick Cooker setting back to Sear at 10 minutes.
  10. Add the chicken broth and shredded chicken.
  11. Let soup simmer for at least 10 minutes, or until ready to eat.
  12. Serve with the optional toppings listed above, and enjoy!
