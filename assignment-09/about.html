Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 bag chocolate chips

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 325oF.
  2. Cream the softened butter, brown sugar, sugar, egg, and vanilla.
  3. Once combined, stir in the dry ingredients.
  4. Roll dough into small balls and place on an ungreased cookie sheet.
  5. Cook the cookies for about 10 minutes or until golden in color.
  6. Remove the cookies and place on a cooling rack.
  7. This recipe makes about 2 dozen cookies.
