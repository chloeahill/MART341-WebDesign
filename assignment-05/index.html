The Song of Achilles

Written by Madeline Miller

The Song of Achilles is an adaotion of the Illiad by Homer and is told in Patroclus' perspective. The book focuses on his relationship with Achilles starting with their first interactions to their time during the Trojan War.

This book is wonderfully written and I would recommend it to anyone looking for something new to read. It tells a beautiful story, but will leave you sobbing by the end.
