Broccoli Beef

Ingredients

Beef and Broccoli Ingredients:


  • 2 cups of cooked white rice
  • 1 lb flank steak, cut into very thin bite-sized strips
  • 2 tbsp olive oil (I like to mix a little sesame oil for flavor as well)
  • 1 lb broccoli, cut into roughly 6 cups of florets
  • Optional - 2 tsp sesame seeds

Stir Fry Sauce Ingredients:


  • 1 tsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tsp minced garlic, roughly 3 cloves
  • 1/2 cup hot water
  • 6 tbsp low sodium soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp packed light brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tbsp corn starch
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil (If you added sesame oil to cook the beef and broccoli, I would recommend using 1 tbsp here!

Directions

  1. Prep: Cook your write rice so it's all ready to go when the rest of the food is done. I would also recommend putting the steak in the freezer for about 30 minutes before so slicing it is easier, but this isn't mandatory.
  2. Combine all of the Stir Fry Sauce ingredients togethe in a small bowl. Make sure all the ingredients are dissolved and set aside.
  3. Grab a large skillet and place it over medium heat. Add roughly 1 tbsp of oil.
  4. Once oil is hot, add the broccoli and sauté for 4-5 minutes. Cook until it is bright green and slightly tender. Then remove broccoli from the pan. If you like your broccoli more soft, add about 2 tbsp of water to the skillet.
  5. Increase the heat to high and add remaining oil. Once that is hot, add the sliced steak in a single layer. Cook the beef for about 2 minutes on each side, or until cooked through.
  6. Once the meat is cook, reduce the heat to medium/low. Add the sauce and simmer for about 3-4 minutes, or until it thickens. Add broccoli and stir it all together.
  7. Serve over white rice and enjoy!





Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 2 packages of lasagna noodles
  • 1-2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cups of yellow onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of minced garlic
  • 2 lbs of ground beef
  • 4 16 oz cans of stewed tomatoes
  • 2 16 oz cans of tomato sauce
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 3 tsp oregano
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 1 1/2 cups of ricatta (or cottage cheese)
  • 4 cups of mozzarella cheese
  • parmasean cheese, for topping

Directions

  1. Prep: If you buy noodles that need to be prepared before assembling the lasanga, do this now. If not, you can move onto the next step.
  2. In a large pot over medium high heat, add the oil and sauté the diced onions until soft. Then add the minced garlic.
  3. Once garlic is fragrant, add the ground beef. Cook until brown and then drain out the fat.
  4. Add the stewed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and seasonings. Mix until well combined and let simmer for at least 20 minutes, but can be for as long as you like.
  5. While the sauce is simmering, add the ricatta and mozzarella to a large bowl. Set aside until you are ready to prepare the lasagna.
  6. Once you are ready to assemble your lasagna, first preheat the oven to 350o F. Then grease a very large baking dish.
  7. To layer the lasagna, first place a layer of sauce, then add a layer noodles, and finally a layer of the cheese mixture. Repeat this until your final layer, which will be just sauce and cheese.
  8. Bake the lasagna for 1 hour, covered. Then bake for 10 minutes, uncovered.
  9. Once noodles are cooked and the cheese is golden, remove from the oven and let sit for several minutes. Then serve and enjoy!






Air Fryer Pork Egg Rolls

Ingredients

  • 40 egg roll wrappers
  • 3 oz vermicelli rice noodles
  • 3 tbsp oil (divided)
  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 1 medium yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 10 oz of shredded carrots
  • 4 cups green cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 1-2 tsp salt, to taste
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 1/2 tbsp low sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • small cup of water
  • Optional - cooking spray or oil when frying.

Directions

  1. Prep: Prepare the rice noodles as instructed on the package. Once they are cooked, and have cooled, chop them to be about 1-inch pieces and place in a large bowl.
  2. In a large skillet over medium/high heat, add 1 tbsp oil and sauté the pork until completely cooked. Move the pork to the bowl with noodles.
  3. In the same skillet, add 2 tbsp oil and sauté the diced onions and shredded carrots. Cook until soft. Then, add the cabbage and cook just until wilted (about 2 minutes). Transfer veggies to the bowl with the pork and noodles.
  4. Season the pork, noodle, and veggie mixture with black pepper, soy sauce, sesame oil, and salt to taste. Mix until fully combined and add anything more to taste.
  5. On a clean surface, lay out 1 egg roll wrapper in a diamond shape. Add about 1/4 cup of mixture onto the bottom third of the wrapper. Fold the bottom corner over the filling and tuck it. Then roll the covered mixture titghtly towards the center and fold in the sides. add a little water to the top corner of the wrapper and finish rolling it to seal. Place the egg roll aside and repeat till mixture is gone.
  6. Preheat the air fryer to 400o F. Place egg rolls in without layering them. If you want, lightly coat in oil and cook the egg rolls for about 6 minutes or until golden and crispy. Flip and repeat until crispy on both sides. Set the cooked egg rolls aside and repeat until all egg rolls are cooked.
  7. Serve immediately with your favorite sauces and enjoy!
