About



Hi there! My name is Chloe Hill and I'm from Red Lodge, Montana. This is where I grew up before moving to Missoula, Montana to attend the University of Montana. Here I am going to school for Media Arts while also minoring in Business Administration. I'll be graduating fall 2023 and I can't wait to start working in my field!

When I was younger and still living in Red Lodge, I started to learn how to cook and bake. It became a fun way to spend time with friends and family. And who doesn't like having yummy food? Now I live in Missoula with my girlfriend. Even though we have a tiny little kitchen, we still cook and bake a lot. All the recipes on this website are ones we have made despite being on a student budget and living in a small apartment. Some of these recipes call for equipment that I know not everyone has, but these tools are not mandatory and can made differently. And with that, I hope you enjoy the recipes!
