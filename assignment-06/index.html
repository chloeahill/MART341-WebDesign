Copy-Cat Olive Garden Zuppa

Ingredients:

  • 1 lbs Italian sausage
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/8 to 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 2 cartons chicken broth
  • 5 large potatoes, sliced thinly
  • at least 1 stalk of kale
  • 2 to 3 slices of crispy bacon (Optional)
  • 1 cup of heavy cream

Directions:

  1. Brown the sausage. Once cooked, remove as much grease as desired.
  2. Add the red pepper flakes and onion. Cook until onion is soft, then add the garlic.
  3. Cook until garlic is fragrant.
  4. Then add the chicken broth and potatoes.
  5. Bring the soup to a boil and cook until potatoes are soft.
  6. Add pieces of kale leaves, diced bacon, and cream.
  7. Enjoy!
